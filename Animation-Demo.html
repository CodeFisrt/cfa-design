Lohagad Fort

Lohagad Fort, literally meaning iron fort, is a historical citadel perched atop a hillock at an elevation of 3400 feet. Situated in the Sahyadri range of Lonavala, it separates the Indrayani basin from the Pawna basin.

Lonaval lake

Built in the year 1876, the Lonavala Lake was a part of the conservation project initiated by the TATA Electric Company.

The aim behind the construction of this lake was to preserve the catchment areas of Shirota and Valvan reservoirs. The main source of water for the lake is River Indrayani.
